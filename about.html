About Us

OddsKraken is a financial analytics platform dedicated to providing investors with the tools and information they need to make informed decisions about their investments. Our mission is to help investors achieve their financial goals through ethical, data-driven investing.

Based in Miami, Florida, OddsKraken was founded by a team of experts in finance and technology who saw a need for a better way for investors to navigate the complex world of investing. Our team is committed to providing our users with the most up-to-date information on stocks and cryptocurrencies, as well as the broader economic landscape.
